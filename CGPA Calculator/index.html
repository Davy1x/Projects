What is CGPA and Why is it Important

CGPA is a widely used metric to evaluate a student's academic performance. It's a calculation of the average grade points earned by a student across all their courses. A good CGPA can open doors to new opportunities, scholarships, and even career prospects. Our CGPA calculator helps you stay on top of your academic game by providing you with an accurate calculation of your CGPA.
